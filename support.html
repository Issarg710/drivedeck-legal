Frequently Asked Questions

Which OBD adapters are supported?

DriveDeck is designed to work with ELM327-compatible Bluetooth OBD adapters. Compatibility may vary depending on vehicle manufacturer and adapter quality.

Does DriveDeck require an internet connection?

No. Most features work completely offline.

Where is my data stored?

All telemetry, trip and analytics data are stored locally on your device. DriveDeck currently does not use a cloud backend.

Why are some vehicle metrics unavailable?

Vehicle manufacturers expose different telemetry data through OBD. Available metrics depend on vehicle support and adapter capabilities.

Why is Range Intelligence unavailable?

Range estimates require sufficient driving and fuel telemetry data to build reliable calculations. Drive more and collect additional telemetry to improve accuracy.
