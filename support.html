Compatibility

DriveDome is currently available for iPhone and optimized for modern iOS devices. Advanced telemetry features require a compatible Bluetooth ELM327 OBD adapter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which OBD adapters are supported?

DriveDome is designed to work with ELM327-compatible Bluetooth OBD adapters. Compatibility may vary depending on vehicle, adapter and ECU support.

Does DriveDome require an internet connection?

No. Most DriveDome features work locally on the device.

Where is my data stored?

Telemetry, trip and analytics data are stored locally on your device. DriveDome currently does not use a cloud backend.

Why are some vehicle metrics unavailable?

Vehicle manufacturers expose different telemetry data through OBD. Available metrics depend on vehicle support and adapter capabilities.

Why is Range Intelligence unavailable?

Range estimates require sufficient fuel and driving telemetry data. Drive more with telemetry enabled to improve calibration.
