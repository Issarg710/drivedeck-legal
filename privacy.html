Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

DriveDeck is designed with privacy in mind. All vehicle, telemetry, trip and analytics data are stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly exported by the user.

Information Collected

DriveDeck may access:

  • Location data (for maps, speed estimation and trip recording)
  • Vehicle telemetry data from a compatible OBD adapter
  • App preferences and settings stored locally

Data Storage

DriveDeck does not operate a backend server. Telemetry and trip information remain on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

DriveDeck may use Apple frameworks such as MapKit, CoreLocation and CoreBluetooth.

Data Sharing

DriveDeck does not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties.
