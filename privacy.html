PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

DriveDeck is designed with privacy in mind. All vehicle, telemetry, trip and analytics data are stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly exported by the user.

2. Information Collected

DriveDeck may access:

  • Location data for maps, speed estimation and trip recording.
  • Vehicle telemetry data from a compatible OBD adapter.
  • Motion sensor data for G-force and cockpit-related features.
  • App preferences and settings stored locally.

3. Data Storage

DriveDeck does not operate a backend server. Telemetry, trip history, fuel history, range data and user preferences remain on the user's device.

4. Data Sharing

DriveDeck does not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties. DriveDeck does not upload vehicle telemetry to a server.

5. Third-Party Services

DriveDeck may use Apple frameworks such as MapKit, CoreLocation, CoreBluetooth and CoreMotion to provide maps, location, Bluetooth connectivity and motion-based features.

6. Local Data Deletion

Users can clear locally stored telemetry data, drive journal data, fuel history and trip information from the app settings.
